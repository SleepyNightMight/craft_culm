How to use:

1. Create item and recipes with scriptable objects. Right click on menu, scriptable objets.
2. Asign icons to the item object.
3. Asign the item ingredients to the recipe object.
4. Drag the crafting inventory prefab into your canvas.
5. Drag the item prefab into the item box found in your crafting inventory prefab.
6. Asign your item data variable in your item prefab.
